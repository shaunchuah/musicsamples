EDTA Plasma Extraction

Equipment:
  • Refrigerated centrifuge(s)
  • 15ml sterile tubes
  • 2ml screw-top tubes
  • QR labels
  • Pipette and 1ml tips
Steps:
  1. 1000 x g for 10mins at room temp
  2. Transfer plasma to sterile tube
  3. 3000 x g for 10mins at room temp
  4. Divide into 1 ml aliquots in screw-top tubes
  5. Label and register each aliquot G-Trac
  6. Freeze at -80°C

  • 1.5ml eppendorf tubes
  • 2ml screw-top tubes
  • QR labels
  • Pipette and 1ml tips

Steps:
  1. 1,600 x g for 10 mins at room temp
  2. Transfer plasma into new tube leaving ~1cm buffer between cells and plasma
  3. Record haemolysis levels
  4. 16,000 x g for 10mins at room temp
  5. Divide into 1 ml aliquots in screw-top tubes
  6. Label and register each aliquot onto G-Trac
  7. Freeze at -80°C
